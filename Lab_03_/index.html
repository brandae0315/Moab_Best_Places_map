About this map

This map shows the most direct route between four of the best places to visit in Moab, Utah. This map shows a path you may take at the end of your day after visting Arches National Park on your way back to your beautiful hotel room at the Sunflower Hill Inn. Stops along the way include the famous Moab Rock Shop, and the delicious Sultan Mediterranean Grill!
